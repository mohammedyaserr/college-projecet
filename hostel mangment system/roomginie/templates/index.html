MES Asmabi Hostel
with Nature

The MES Asmabi College Hostel offers a comfortable and secure living environment for students, featuring spacious rooms, Wi-Fi, 24/7 security, and a nutritious mess. Located within the college campus, it provides easy access to college activities while fostering a supportive atmosphere for academic and personal growth. The hostel promotes community and camaraderie among residents, ensuring a peaceful space for study and rest.

We Serve Fresh and
Delicious Food

The MES Asmabi College Hostel offers comfortable, well-maintained rooms with basic furniture and essential amenities, providing a peaceful environment for students. The hostel’s mess serves nutritious, freshly prepared meals that cater to diverse tastes and dietary needs. This combination of quality accommodation and wholesome food creates an ideal space for students to live, study, and thrive.
